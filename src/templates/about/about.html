Adam Goldberg is a Peabody, Emmy, and Clio winning producer who has worked on some of the biggest sports documentaries of the last decade. After starting as a Production Assistant at ESPN, Adam is now Showrunning series and documentaries for SDF as well as other production companies, brands, networks, and streaming services all around the world.

Toni Legaspi came up in the world of business management working with high profile luminaries, athletes, and celebrities on all of their financial needs. SDF is very lucky to have Toni as its CFO, as her attention to detail as well as her discretion, make her the perfect fit for television production.
